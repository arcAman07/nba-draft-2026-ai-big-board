## Sourced scouting observations (human scouts, cited)

- Babcock Hoops credits Carr with "smooth mechanics off the catch and the dribble; confident from deep," says he "elevates well at the rim; capable of explosive finishes in transition and in the halfcourt," and cautions that he "needs to fill out his frame to handle contact on both ends." Source, Babcock Hoops, https://www.babcockhoops.com/post/2026-nba-draft-cameron-carr-scouting-report
- Sports Illustrated's draft newsfeed report describes a wing who can "operate as a shot-making threat with clean catch-and-shoot mechanics and the ability to elevate into jumpers off the dribble," giving him real three-level scoring potential. Source, Sports Illustrated, https://www.si.com/nba/draft/newsfeed/nba-draft-scouting-report-baylor-wing-cameron-carr
- At The Hive's prospect report cites his 2026 combine measurements, 6'4.5" barefoot, 184.4 pounds, 7'0.75" wingspan, and a 42.5-inch max vertical, projecting him as a 3-and-D wing who can space a starting unit, attack closeouts in a straight line, and rise above the rim on cuts and putbacks, while noting the skinny frame is the main developmental flag. Source, At The Hive, https://atthehive.com/2026/05/20/charlotte-hornets-prospect-scouting-report-cameron-carr/
