## Sourced scouting observations (human scouts, cited)

- Sports Business Classroom's scouting piece notes Graves played about 22.6 minutes as Santa Clara's sixth man, producing 11.8 points, 6.5 rebounds, 1.9 steals, and 0.9 blocks on 51 percent from the floor and 41 percent from three, calling his most reliable offensive translation the combination of shooting and offensive rebounding. Source, Sports Business Classroom, https://sportsbusinessclassroom.com/scouting-santa-clara-forward-allen-graves/
- Babcock Hoops highlights truly remarkable defensive playmaking instincts for his size and position, citing roughly a 5.3 steal percentage and 5.3 block percentage as an extremely rare combination for a freshman forward, while cautioning that he is not super mobile or bouncy, part of why he finished only about 52 percent of his layups. Source, Babcock Hoops, https://www.babcockhoops.com/post/2026-nba-draft-allen-graves-scouting-report
- At The Hive, the Charlotte Hornets team blog, profiles Graves as a 6'9", roughly 225 pound forward with intriguing all-around value, a three-level scorer with soft touch around the basket, good feel as a passer, and encouraging shooting upside for a team that won 26 games and returned to the NCAA Tournament. Source, At The Hive, https://atthehive.com/2026/06/08/charlotte-hornets-prospect-scouting-report-allen-graves/
