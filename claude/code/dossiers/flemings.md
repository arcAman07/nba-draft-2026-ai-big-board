## Verdict

Flemings is the board's quiet model favorite, ranked 4th by the numbers on the strength of a 32.6 AST%, the group's best free-throw percentage, and a defense-inclusive BPM, all wrapped in the fastest agility testing in this class.
His cohort tells you exactly what the bet is, Fox and Rose at the top, a 20% bust rate at the bottom, and a thin 183-pound frame with the group's shortest wingspan in between.
The shooting volume is low and the mechanics draw critiques, so the jumper is the swing skill.
At pick 6 on my board you are paying a Tier 2 price for the one Tier 2 guard whose cohort contains genuine stars, and that asymmetry is worth one spot over Acuff.
