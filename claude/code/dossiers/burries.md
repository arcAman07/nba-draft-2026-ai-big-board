## Verdict

Burries is the cleanest risk-adjusted buy in the 9-12 range: a 39-game season at 61.6 TS%, real defense on the frame-based and scouted evidence, and a comp cohort that has literally never produced a bust.
The model's rank of 16 is not a disagreement about whether he plays, only about how high the ceiling goes, and a 6.7% cohort All-Star rate says the star case is thin.
At 9 I am paying for certainty in a class whose middle is full of variance, and for a two-way guard who fits a contender or a rebuild equally well.
If he never becomes more than Arron Afflalo with a better handle, the pick still returns value; if the Kennard-grade shooting fully translates, it returns a starter.
