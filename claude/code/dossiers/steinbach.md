## Verdict

Steinbach is the class's best rebounder and one of its most reliable producers, an 18.5 and 11.8 freshman season on 63.6 TS% at a position where that usually translates. The tension is entirely on defense, the frames and the scouts agree the base, hands, and second effort are real, and they also agree the block rate and lateral quickness are not. His cohort is high-variance in both directions, a 26.7% All-Star rate against a 20% bust rate, which is why the model (22) and the market (16.5) disagree. At 18 I am paying for the glass and the Bosh-shaped tail while accepting that he may never anchor a defense.
