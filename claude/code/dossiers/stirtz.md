## Verdict

Stirtz is the cleanest scouts-versus-model fight on my board, the model says 33 and quarter-odds bust, the market says 19.5 and best feel in the class. Everything except his birthdate argues for him, 84.8% from the line, 51.7% catch-and-shoot, a 24.9 AST% against 10.1 TOV%, and frames that show a high, repeatable release with a composed handle. Everything about his birthdate argues against him, at 22.73 with ordinary testing he has to beat NBA athletes with skill alone, and only some guards in his cohort did. I rank him 22 because the Brunson and Devin Harris outcomes are exactly the tail a late-first pick should chase, but the 20% cohort bust rate is the reason he is not in the teens.
