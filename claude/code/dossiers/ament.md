## Verdict

Ament is the most honest disagreement on this board, the model calls him a rotation-or-bust player at rank 25 while his historical cohort of teenage 6'10 shot-creators (Barnes floor, Tatum ceiling, 33% All-Star rate, 7% bust rate) says he is the kind of player models systematically miss.
The 2025-26 inputs really were poor, a 53.4 TS% on 29 usage with bad rim finishing on a frame that needs years of weight work.
But the things that cannot be faked, the 9'1.5" reach, the low live-dribble crouch at 6'10, the 79% line on seven free throws a game, are the raw material of the cohort's stars.
Ranking him 8th prices in real bust risk while refusing to let a feature-blind regression have the last word on the class's only teenage jumbo creator.
