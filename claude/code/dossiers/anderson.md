## Verdict

Anderson is the best pure value bet of my eight, a 20-year-old who just ran a Big 12 offense at a 35.2 AST% while shooting 41.5% on nearly eight threes a game. The model puts him 15th and his cohort pairs a 6.9 median VORP with a 6.7% bust rate, numbers that simply do not belong at consensus 21. I rank him 19 because the production says lottery-adjacent and the body says late first, and splitting that gap still leaves value on the table for whoever takes him.
