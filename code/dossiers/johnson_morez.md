## Verdict

Johnson is the cleanest agreement pick on my board, consensus 17, model 14, my rank 17. The profile is a 250-pound defensive big with genuinely rare movement testing, near-elite finishing efficiency, and the lowest bust probability in this range. The cohort says the median outcome is modest, an energy big who hovers around replacement, but the Adebayo tail is the reason he goes in the teens rather than the twenties. If the 78.2% foul shooting foreshadows a usable spot-up three, the outcome distribution shifts a full tier. Drafting him at 15-17 prices the floor and gets the tail for free.
