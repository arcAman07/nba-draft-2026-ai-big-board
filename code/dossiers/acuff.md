## Verdict

Acuff was the best volume scorer among the guards in this class, 23.5 a game at 44% from three with a better than 3-to-1 assist-to-turnover profile, and the 49-point Alabama film shows a real pull-up architecture behind the numbers.
The problem is everything around the scoring, a 0.7 defensive BPM, scout reports that he dies on screens, a below-the-rim finishing profile, and the highest bust probability (0.110) of the top seven on this board.
His cohort says the median outcome is a solid scoring guard, not a star, and the ceiling comps top out at Devin Harris rather than Fox or Rose.
He is a worthy swing at 7 for a team that needs buckets at the point, but within Tier 2 the evidence puts him a notch behind Flemings.
