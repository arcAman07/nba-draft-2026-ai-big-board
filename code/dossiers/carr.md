## Verdict

Carr is my board's biggest fade, consensus 15 against my 23, and the reasoning is in the splits. The shooting package is genuine, a high repeatable release confirmed on film, 37.4% on volume, 80.1% from the line, and the best vertical and lane agility of my eight wrapped in a 7-foot wingspan. But he is 21.6 with a 14.6 AST%, a 0.274 model bust probability, and a frame the close-ups show as wiry in a league that targets thin wings, and his cohort's median outcome was 0.4 VORP. The 3-and-D outcome is live and his cohort almost never fully busted, so 23 is not a dismissal, it is a refusal to pay a mid-teens price for one translatable skill.
