## Verdict

Dybantsa is the class's clearest bet on scoring talent, 25.5 points per game as a 19-year-old wing with a 42-inch max vert and a near-unanimous market No. 1 ranking.
The model's skepticism is specific and legible, his efficiency did not keep pace with his enormous usage, and his BPM sits well below Boozer's and Peterson's, which drags his median outcome down even while his All-Star probability stays second-best in the class.
His cohort says the floor is very safe (0% bust) but stardom hit only 20% of the time.
He stays in Tier 1 at No. 2, behind Boozer on evidence and ahead of everyone else on ceiling.
