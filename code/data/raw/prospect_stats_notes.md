## Global caveats / unverified items
- Handedness: NOT systematically collected for any prospect (UNVERIFIED for all). Not a column in the CSV; no reliable single source was fetched for it.
- International prospects (Karim Lopez, Sergio De Larrea, Luigi Suigo): usage rate, AST%, TOV%, OBPM/DBPM/BPM are UNVERIFIED (RealGM does not publish these for international leagues). TS% was computed by hand from RealGM season totals: PTS / (2*(FGA + 0.44*FTA)).
- Injury notes for ranks 26-40 were not researched in depth (thin rows per task spec), except Richie Saunders whose season-ending ACL tear was verified. 'not researched in depth' in the CSV means no injury search was run, not that the player was healthy.
- For top-25 college players with full games-played counts, 'none significant found' is an inference from a full season of games plus no injury stories surfacing in searches for the flagged players; it is not an exhaustive medical history.
- Heights/weights for college players are the school-listed values from Sports-Reference player pages (not combine measurements). Tankathon lists combine-style measurements that differ slightly.
- Class year is from the Sports-Reference 2025-26 row (FR/SO/JR/SR).
- Stats basis: NCAA players = full 2025-26 season from Sports-Reference per-game and advanced tables. Karim Lopez = 2025-26 NBL Australia (30 games). Sergio De Larrea = 2025-26 all competitions (EuroLeague + ACB + cups, 65 games); ACB-only and EuroLeague-only splits noted below. Luigi Suigo = 2025-26 ABA Liga only (26 games); all-competition numbers noted below.
