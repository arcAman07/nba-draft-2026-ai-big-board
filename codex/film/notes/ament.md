## Sourced scouting observations (human scouts, cited)

- Floor & Ceiling's scouting report describes a "6-foot-10 wing with solid size, feel, shooting, and defensive tools," but flags the finishing numbers, "51.4% (57/111) at the rim" and "46.9% (46/98) on layups is concerning," and notes his three-point percentage (32.4% on 111 attempts) "needs to tick up"; defensively he "moves around the floor well, even if he isn't explosive. Chases. Stays in front. Disciplined." Source, Floor & Ceiling, https://floorandceiling.substack.com/p/nate-ament-scouting-report
- Sports Illustrated's draft profile notes that at a confirmed 6'10" in shoes Ament has legitimate NBA size and length that "rears its head across his game," helping him shoot over defenders and underpinning both his floor and ceiling on defense, with a level of mobility and coordination that jumps out for his size. Source, Sports Illustrated, https://www.si.com/nba/draft/prospect-profiles/nba-draft-scouting-report-tennessee-nate-ament
- Babcock Hoops lists Ament's major strengths as his size and fluidity, face-up scoring upside, rebounding, and defensive upside, while treating him as one of the more polarizing evaluations in the class because the efficiency has not yet matched the tools. Source, Babcock Hoops, https://www.babcockhoops.com/post/2026-nba-draft-nate-ament-scouting-report
